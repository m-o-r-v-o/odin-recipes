Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is some good food.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Red Sauce
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef
  2. Mix beef with red sauce
  3. Make layers in pan, ordered with pasta on bottom, meat/sauce, then cheese. Make as many layers as you'd like
  4. Bake in oven at 400 F for one hour
  5. Enjoy!
